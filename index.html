This game will allow you to clear your mind and become focused and concentrated!
All you have to do is:
(*) press the LEFT button if the current circle is different from the previous circle's color
(*) press the RIGHT button if the current circle matches the previous circle's color
(*) for the first 'START' circle, you can press either LEFT or RIGHT keys to get started!
(*) if you are on a cell phone or touch screen, you can just tap the LEFT/RIGHT buttons!
